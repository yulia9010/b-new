01

Look-alike Model

brody.ai look-alike models anatomize either people who are interested in a product or your brands customers to find the commonalities within other data sets. This helps to find highly qualified customers who previously would have been difficult to identify in a sea of data. Just as important brody.ai look-alike model can tell you which leads are not like consumers who have either expressed interest or converted.

There are two methods we use to develop look-alike machine learning models:

  • COMPONENT 1

    Natural Language Processing

  • COMPONENT 2

    Conversion Look-alike Models

COMPONENT 1

Natural Language Processing

  • Utilizing Natural Language Processing we target online search keyword trends, buyer behavior on social networks, and websites that interest the target audience.

  • Next, we obtain the cookies of that audience to identify their demographics and track them with a re-targeting pixel. We then input that information into our algorithm and apply machine learning.

  • Lastly, the results are applied to the Client’s database allowing us to extract only those customers that look like the audience of people interested in the target industry.

COMPONENT 2

Conversion Look-alike Models

  • Brands looking to identity specific users that convert can provide us with conversation data to build a custom look-alike model specific to their products.

  • This information is then used to build a custom lookalike model designed to identify a highly targeted audience comprised of consumers that share similar attributes as the conversion set.

02

Predictive Model

Picking up where the Look-alike Model leaves off, brody.ai’s Predictive Model uses machine learning to further refine the WHO and adds the WHAT and WHEN.

Using brody.ai’s Predictive Model, you can better understand the emotional triggers, preferences, likes and dislikes of your customers, even down to their word preferences and the time of day they are most likely to notice your creative.

  • COMPONENT 1

    Understand Your Customers

  • COMPONENT 2

COMPONENT 1

Understand Your Customers

  • The Predictive Model helps you not only understand who your potential customers are, but what messages, language, images, and other creative elements resonate with them.

  • Brody.ai uses machine learning to suggest emotional triggers in your marketing campaigns.

COMPONENT 2

Knowing When...

  • Timing is everything — We can predict the best time to contact. Further, brody.ai can determine the optimal time to deliver your creatives to maximize the likelihood of engagement. All automatically.

  • Each person receives the right message with highly relevant content at the exact right time to entice engagement.
